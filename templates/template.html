Demographic parity

The algorithmic output should be independent of group membership

Equalized odds


The algorithmic output should be independent of group membership when conditioned on the presence of the disease



The sensitivity and specificity should be similar across groups

Predictive parity


The probability of the disease should be independent of group membership when conditioned upon the outcome



Translation: the positive and negative predictive values should be similar across groups
